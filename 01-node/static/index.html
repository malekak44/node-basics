Here's a time line of Kim Taehung's life:

  • V aka Kim Tae-hyung (Korean: 김태형) was born on December 30, 1995 in Daegu, South Korea, and grew up in Geochang Country.
  • He is the eldest of three children, with a younger brother and sister.
  • V first aspired to be a professional singer in elementary school.
  • And eventually began taking saxophone lessons in early middle school as a means of pursuing the career.
  • V's father agreed with his journey on learning how to play the saxophone.
  • V initially became a trainee for Big Hit Entertainment after passing an audition in Daegu.
  • After graduating from Korean Arts High School in 2014, V enrolled in Global Cyber University.
